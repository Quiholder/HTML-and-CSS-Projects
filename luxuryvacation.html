Ora Beach

Ora beach in settled in the village of Saleman, Northern Seram District, Central Maluku. On this beach there is a resort named Ora Beach Resort which has 6 pieces cottages and all made of wood with thatched roofs. Ora Beach is a hidden paradise located in North of Seram Island – Indonesia. A secluded beach with only private eco-resort and surrounded by giant limestone hills, truly an excellent place to find serenity.

Reduit Beach

Reduit is the most beautiful beach on the island of Pigeon on the north coast of the island state of Saint Lucia. This is an elite resort, which is included in the “Golden Mile” beaches of the coast.
